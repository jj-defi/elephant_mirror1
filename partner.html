About the Partner Network

Our Partner Network, aka "The Herd", rewards participants for onboarding their direct social networks where they have maximum reach and influence. We believe in organic tierless teams. Recruit as many to partner with you as you want. Encourage them to do the same. Partner rewards are shared.

Partner Rewards are split 50/50 on every deposit and roll:
- 1% rewards on deposit
- 1% rewards on roll

Eligibility and Requirements:
- rewards are currently only available for Stampede
- accounts must have over 100 TRUNK bonded to be eligible for reward
- both partners must meet account minimums before distribution begins

Change your upline “partner” at anytime. You are not required to have one at all, and there’s no default wallet earning rewards. Team airdrops downward are automatic due to the 50/50 split. If you setup a partner you will receive bonus rewards of 0.5% on deposits and 0.5% on rolls. The more team members that choose you as a partner, the more you make.

Everyone earns bonuses in a first of its kind system. Stampede is 100% solvent and sustainable. Use the "Partner" tab to set your upline partner. Use "Lookup" tab to verify a team member.
