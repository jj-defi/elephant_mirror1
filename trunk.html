About

ELEPHANT.MONEY STABLE is a future-proof way to compound profits made across your portfolio. Lock in your gains. Use the Reserve to mint Elephant Money Stable (TRUNK). TRUNK is over-collateralized by BUSD with deep liquidity. ELEPHANT is used to generate yield for TRUNK and support the peg. Both collateral assets are securely stored in treasuries. The rest of TRUNK supply can redeemed against an ever growing ELEPHANT Treasury and TRUNK/BUSD LP.

The Elephant Treasury and use cases built around it act as a large/active market participant. It is aimed to absorb the circulating supply of ELEPHANT through minting activity and reflection rewards received from incoming trading volume. This differentiates it from the passive Elephant Graveyard which also protects the price of ELEPHANT. The Elephant Treasury also receives passive reflection rewards which ensures it is able to earn income to buy TRUNK and also add liquidity to the TRUNK/BUSD LP pair. As the Elephant treasury grows through minting, the inflows of the redemption pool will exceed the outflows allowing the system to operate in a steady state.

* APY is based on daily compounding
