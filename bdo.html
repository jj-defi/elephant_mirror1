Mini Guide

ELEPHANT.MONEY is a permissionless system for economic inclusion and helps its community accumulate wealth through STABLE active and passive cash flows.

Transactions

You can deposit and withdraw BDO to participate in passive dividends provided by ELEPHANT.MONEY. A transaction fee of 10% is charged in each direction

 

Fees

8% of every transaction in ELEPHANT.MONEY goes to the dividend pool. 2% is paid out instantly to BDO HODLERS!!! All rewards are paid out 2% daily from the dividend pool.

 

Lifetime Dividends
